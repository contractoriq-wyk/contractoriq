Getting Started

What is DrayageIQ?

DrayageIQ reads your weekly settlement statements and shows you exactly where every dollar goes — gross, net, deductions, and margin — in seconds instead of hours of manual math.

Do I need an account to try it?

No. You can explore Demo Mode with sample data before creating an account. To save your real settlements permanently and sync across devices, you'll need to sign in with your email.

How do I upload a settlement?

Go to the Document Analyzer tab, tap Upload PDF, and select your settlement statement. DrayageIQ reads it in under 30 seconds and extracts every load, deduction, and fuel charge automatically.

Plans & Billing

What's the difference between Standard and Pro Smart?

Standard covers core tracking: unlimited scans, deduction breakdowns, move performance, and manual fuel logging. Pro Smart adds live diesel and weather data, an AI advisor that uses your real numbers, Return on Spend tracking, the Fuel Surcharge Calculator, and the full Growth tab.

Can I try Pro Smart features without upgrading?

Yes. Every Pro Smart feature is visible on Standard, and you get one free trial use of each feature every 31 days — no credit card, no commitment. Look for the "🎁 Try Free" button on any locked feature.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal (linked in your confirmation email) or by contacting support@getdrayageiq.com. You keep access through the end of your paid period.

Your Data

Is my data safe if I switch phones or use a computer?

Yes. Your data is securely stored in our encrypted cloud database and syncs automatically across every device you sign into — phone, tablet, or desktop.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, anytime. Use the "Delete My Data" option in the app menu to permanently and immediately erase your account data.

Does DrayageIQ sell my data?

No. We never sell your personal or business data to anyone. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Using the Tools

How accurate is the Fuel Surcharge Calculator?

It calculates a fair FSC using today's live diesel price and your real truck MPG, compared against a standard baseline. It's a helpful estimate for quoting clients — always use your own judgment on final pricing.

What does "Return on Spend" mean?

It's the ratio of what you earned versus what you spent running your truck that week. A ratio of 1:3 or higher is considered excellent; 1:1.5 is a safe minimum. Below that, your costs may be eating too much into your revenue.

Is the AI advisor giving me financial advice?

No. DrayageIQ, including all AI-generated output, is for informational purposes only and is not financial, tax, or legal advice. Always consult a qualified professional before making business decisions. See our Terms of Service.
