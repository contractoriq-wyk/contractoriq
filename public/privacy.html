Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lilwemma Services Co ("we," "us," or "our"), the operator of DrayageIQ (the "Service," available at getdrayageiq.com), collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

DrayageIQ is operated by Lilwemma Services Co, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Maryland, United States. For privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@getdrayageiq.com.

2. Information We Collect

DrayageIQ is built to keep your business data on your own device. The information involved falls into these categories:

  • Data stored locally on your device. Your settlement details, business profile, expenses, documents, and app preferences are saved in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Account information (if you sign in). If you create an account using Google Sign-In, we receive your name and email address through our authentication provider to identify your account. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Information processed for AI features. When you use AI-powered features (such as document scanning, the AI advisor, or report analysis), the relevant content you submit is transmitted securely to our AI processing provider to generate a response. See Section 4.
  • Payment information (if you subscribe). Payments are handled by our third-party payment processor. We do not collect or store your full card number or bank details.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the Service and its features.
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • To process the content you submit to AI features and return results to you.
  • To respond to your support and privacy requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to operate the Service:

  • AI processing (Anthropic). When you use AI features, your submitted content is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. We do not use your data to train AI models.
  • Authentication (Google and Supabase). Used to sign you in and manage your account session.
  • Hosting (Vercel). Serves the application and standard server logs (such as IP address and request time) for security and reliability.
  • Payments (our payment processor). Processes subscription transactions securely.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Because most of your data is stored locally in your browser, it remains until you clear it. You can remove all locally stored DrayageIQ data at any time using the "Delete My Data" option in the app's menu, or by clearing your browser storage. Account and subscription records, if any, are retained only as long as needed to provide the Service and meet legal and accounting requirements.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live (including California residents under the CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you.
  • Request access to or a copy of that information.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@getdrayageiq.com. For locally stored data, you can also delete it yourself instantly using the in-app "Delete My Data" option.

7. Security

The Service runs over HTTPS, and data submitted to AI features is transmitted over encrypted connections. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for business users 18 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above shows when it was last revised. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.
